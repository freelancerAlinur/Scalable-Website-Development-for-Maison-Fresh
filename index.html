Our Story

Born from a passion for authentic French market culture, Maison Fresh represents more than just a produce store. It's a celebration of the artisanal tradition that has defined French markets for centuries.

Our founders, inspired by countless mornings spent in Parisian markets, recognized Toronto's appetite for genuine quality and sophisticated taste. We're bringing that same commitment to excellence, that same attention to provenance, and that same joy of discovery to St Lawrence Market.

Every item in our collection will be carefully curated, from heirloom tomatoes grown by local artisans to imported French delicacies that transport you to the cobblestone streets of Provence. We believe that exceptional food tells a story, and we're here to share those stories with Toronto.

What to Expect

Discover our carefully curated selection of premium produce and artisanal goods, each chosen for its exceptional quality and authentic French market appeal.

Artisanal Produce

Handpicked seasonal vegetables and fruits from local farms and specialty growers, featuring heirloom varieties and organic selections that celebrate natural flavors.

French Delicacies

Imported cheeses, charcuterie, and specialty items sourced directly from French producers, bringing authentic flavors and traditional craftsmanship to Toronto.

Fresh Herbs & Greens

Living herbs and microgreens grown with care, plus foraged selections that add unique flavors and elevate everyday cooking to gourmet experiences.

Artisan Bakery

Daily-baked breads and pastries crafted using traditional French techniques, featuring sourdough, croissants, and seasonal specialties made with premium ingredients.

Gourmet Pantry

Curated selection of oils, vinegars, and condiments from renowned French producers, plus unique finds that transform simple ingredients into extraordinary meals.

Seasonal Flowers

Fresh seasonal blooms and potted herbs that bring the beauty of French gardens indoors, carefully selected for their fragrance and natural elegance.
